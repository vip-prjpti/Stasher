About Stasher - Revolutionizing Sustainability

How Stasher Bags Help Reduce Plastic Waste

Stasher is on a mission to reduce single-use plastics with sustainable, reusable alternatives. Our silicone bags are a simple, eco-friendly solution for everyday life.

What are Stasher Bags?

Stasher bags are reusable, eco-friendly silicone bags that replace single-use plastic. Made from food-safe silicone, they're perfect for food storage, organizing, and more. Stasher bags are durable, non-toxic, and safe for the environment, offering a greener alternative to plastic bags.

Why Choose Stasher Bags?

Every time you use a Stasher bag, you help reduce plastic waste. Our silicone bags are built to last for years, unlike single-use plastic, which harms wildlife and pollutes the environment. Eco-Friendly Tips for Reducing Plastic Use

  • Switch to Reusable Bags: Use Stasher silicone bags for food storage, travel, and more.
  • Choose Sustainable Containers: Opt for reusable glass or silicone containers.
  • Say No to Single-Use Plastics: Avoid disposable plastic bottles, cups, and straws.

How to Clean a Stasher Bag

  • Dishwasher Safe: Place it on the top rack for easy cleaning.
  • Hand Wash: Use warm, soapy water for a thorough clean.

Are Silicone Bags Safe?

Yes! Stasher bags are made from BPA-free, non-toxic, food-grade silicone. They're safe for use in the microwave, freezer, and oven, providing a safe and durable alternative to plastic.
